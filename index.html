Shauli Productions is an independent production company. We believe the most urgent stories are the ones that resist easy resolution — where love, identity, and survival arrive in the same breath.

Our work is personal before it is commercial. Character-driven, formally bold, and built for audiences who want to feel something they cannot immediately name. Nothing Between Us But Love is our debut feature film.

Tel Aviv, wartime. A dancer and a singer fall into each other — and cannot find their way back out. A love story that asks: when does devotion become erasure?

Shot across multiple formats in a shifting palette of colour and black-and-white, the film moves between heat and stillness, joy and collapse, searching for the moment when love stops saving you.

Visual Language
Impressionistic and tactile. The camera breathes with its subjects. Grainy film textures and fluid handheld movement. Colour and black-and-white used as emotional registers rather than stylistic choice. Directed references: Wong Kar-wai, Terrence Malick, Gaspar Noé.
